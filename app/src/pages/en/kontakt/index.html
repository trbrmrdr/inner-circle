History.

Old walls. Fresh wind.

Originally built around 1200 as a castle with a defence tower, the present castle complex was created in the Baroque period. For centuries, monks of the Einsiedeln monastery inhabited the stately home before it developed into a unique venue for celebrations and seminars.
