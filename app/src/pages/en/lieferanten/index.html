We care for tHE ENVIRONMENT

Sustainable gastronomy operation.

We buy seasonally/locally, save energy, and avoid food waste and waste.

Unfortunately, still too rarely lived reality, but hopefully soon everywhere a matter of course: we clearly commit to handling our environment carefully and resource-efficiently. Behind the scenes, we work on it every day. So, if it tastes a little better than usual - now you know why.
