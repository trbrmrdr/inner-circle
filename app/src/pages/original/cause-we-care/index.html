Caring for Our Planet: Join Us in Protecting the Environment

Sustainable use of resources and environmental protection are close to our hearts. Despite our efforts to minimize our environmental and climate impact, greenhouse gases are inevitably emitted during a stay at the castle.

You can help us actively protect nature and the environment! Through the myclimate «Cause We Care» initiative, we offer you the opportunity to make a voluntary contribution to support impactful environmental and climate protection efforts together with us.

This commitment is realized in collaboration with the Swiss foundation myclimate as part of the «Cause We Care» program.

with CHF 2.50 per overnight stay, you actively support local climate protection measures.

Here’s how It Works:

  1. Your Contribution
    When booking your stay or enjoying our services, you can make a small voluntary contribution to support climate and environmental protection.
  2. Double the Impact
    We match your contribution! Every euro or franc you donate is doubled by us to maximize the impact.
  3. Concrete Actions
    Part of the funds is invested directly into certified climate protection projects through myclimate. The other part is used to implement sustainability measures right here at the castle.

Together, we take meaningful steps to preserve the environment and combat climate change. Thank you for your support!

Climate Protection Measures.

Projects you support with us

The high-quality climate protection projects by myclimate | myclimate Switzerland

  1. Window Replacement at Inner Circle
    All windows at Inner Circle are being gradually replaced. The old windows in the building are no longer airtight, leading to significant heat loss and a much higher demand for heating energy than necessary. New windows with a sun protection factor are being installed to prevent heat intrusion during the summer.
  2. Eco-Friendly Cleaning Products
    In our housekeeping operations, we use cleaning products from EM-Kraft, ensuring environmentally responsible, professional cleaning based on microorganisms. By 2025, we aim to extend this sustainable solution to all areas, including the kitchen and service. Learn more about the company and its products at www.emkraft.ch.
  3. Promoting Public Transport Use
    Inner Circle is not directly accessible by public transport. Employees traveling by train must walk approximately 20–30 minutes from Eschenz station to the castle. Through our project "Saving Car Kilometers Together," we encourage alternatives such as carpooling, e-bikes, walking, or cycling. At the end of the year, the collective success in reducing CO2 emissions through these alternatives will be celebrated with a team outing.

Our projects are regularly updated and adapted to current needs. A dedicated project team analyzes our operations every two months to develop new measures.
