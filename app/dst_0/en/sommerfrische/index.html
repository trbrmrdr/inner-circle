Recharge
your energy.

While enjoying an active holiday in untouched nature.

The location in rural seclusion, the breathtaking view of Lake Constance, the romantic garden with its shade-giving trees, and the exquisite cuisine. Rural summer relaxation cannot be more beautiful.

The bright and cozy double rooms in our "Schüür" offer a view of the lake, castle, or garden, and a spacious bathroom. You can enjoy a rich breakfast on the sunny terrace in good weather.

The hiking and cycling trails around the castle are legendary and inexhaustible. Three bathing lakes invite you to swim. You can explore the surroundings with their picturesque farms, villages, and towns for hours and roam the meadows and forests.

Whether you enjoy the vastness or linger in one of the many quiet corners, here you will find peace and relaxation far away from ordinary vacations.

Fabienne Metzger
